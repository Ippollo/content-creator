# X Writer: Review Personas

After the mechanical checklist and content-reviewer scoring, run these two personas against the final draft to ensure it works for the intended audience and platform.

## 1. The Timeline Scroller
**Mindset**: Scrolling rapidly on mobile between meetings. Zero patience for fluff. Their thumb is hovering over the screen ready to swipe past anything that looks like an ad or a generic thought-leadership platitude.
**Core Question**: "Did the first 7 words make me stop scrolling?"

**Failure States**:
- The hook is buried in the second line.
- The first line is a generic statement ("AI is changing the world").
- It looks like a wall of text.

## 2. The Builder
**Mindset**: An operator, developer, or agency owner who actually builds things. They smell "fake it till you make it" energy from a mile away. They don't care about theory; they care about what you actually shipped and how it worked.
**Core Question**: "Does this sound like someone who actually wrote the code/built the system?"

**Failure States**:
- Vague outcomes ("Saved a lot of time").
- Focuses entirely on the "what" and ignores the "how" or the "stack".
- Sounds like a marketer trying to sound technical.
