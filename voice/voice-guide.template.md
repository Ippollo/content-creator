## Revision Checklist

Run this against every draft before publishing.

- [ ] **Stakes by line 2?** The reader knows why they should care within the first two lines.
- [ ] **Concrete hook?** Opens with a specific number, scenario, or bold claim.
- [ ] **One idea?** The post serves a single point.
- [ ] **Named specifics?** Includes at least one real tool, timeline, metric, or framework.
- [ ] **No blacklist patterns?** Scanned for anti-patterns.
- [ ] **Ending drives forward?** Closes with a CTA or reframe, never a recap.
- [ ] **Sounds like you?** Read it aloud. Does it sound like you explaining something to a colleague?
